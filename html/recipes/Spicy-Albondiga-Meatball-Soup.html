Spicy Albondigas Meatball Soup

Description

A bold and comforting Mexican meatball soup, infused with a smoky, spicy broth made from chipotle and guajillo peppers. Packed with hearty vegetables, herbs, and tender albondigas (meatballs), it's the perfect one-pot meal for chilly days.

Broth Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 dried guajillo peppers
  • 6 oz chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • ½ cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can (24 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 tsp ground cloves
  • 8 cups beef broth (plus 2-3 more cups if needed)

Instructions – Broth

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the guajillo peppers. Boil for 15 minutes. Rinse under cool water, remove stems, and discard the seeds.
  2. In a large soup pot, heat olive oil over medium-low. Add chopped onions and sauté for 8–12 minutes, until softened. Stir in minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. While onions are cooking, blend the softened guajillo peppers, chipotle peppers, vinegar, cumin, oregano, paprika, crushed tomatoes, and 4 cups of beef broth until smooth.
  4. Pour the blended mixture into the soup pot. Add remaining broth, cinnamon sticks, and ground cloves. Stir in the cooked onions and garlic. Simmer gently while preparing the meatballs.

Meatball Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • ¼ cup long grain white rice
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Vegetables

  • 3 large carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 3–4 yellow or golden potatoes, chopped
  • 2 zucchini, chopped

Instructions – Meatballs & Final Soup

  1. In a large bowl, combine beef, pork, egg, onion, rice, garlic, cumin, garlic powder, pepper, and oregano. Mix just until combined, do not overmix.
  2. Form the mixture into 2-inch meatballs.
  3. Gently place the meatballs into the simmering broth. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the carrots, potatoes, and zucchini to the soup. Boil for 5 minutes, then reduce heat to medium-low.
  5. If needed, add 2–3 more cups of beef broth to keep everything well-covered. Simmer for 20–25 minutes, or until vegetables are tender and meatballs are fully cooked.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.

To Serve

Top each bowl with freshly chopped cilantro and a generous squeeze of lime juice. Serve with warm tortillas or rice if desired.

Storage Notes

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days
