Cowboy Butter Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 1 raw chicken breast (boneless, skinless)
  • 1–2 tbsp butter
  • 1–2 tsp Cowboy Butter seasoning (adjust to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Prep the chicken: Pat dry and slice in half if thick.
  2. Season: Rub with Cowboy Butter seasoning. Let sit 5–10 minutes.
  3. Heat the pan: Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook: Sear chicken 5–7 mins per side until golden & cooked to 165°F (74°C).
  5. Optional basting: Spoon melted butter over chicken during last 2 minutes.
  6. Rest: Let sit 5 minutes before slicing.
Pro tip: Add garlic or lemon juice to the butter for extra flavor!
