Veggie Delight Skillet

Description

A vibrant and hearty vegetable medley sautéed to perfection with garlic, herbs, and a surprising twist of apricot jam. This dish is earthy, sweet, and packed with flavor, perfect as a main or a side.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup baby carrots
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup asparagus tips
  • 1 cup rutabaga, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup parsnips, peeled and sliced
  • ½ red onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tablespoons apricot jam
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: fresh thyme or rosemary for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Prep all vegetables by washing, peeling (if needed), and slicing them evenly.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, add olive oil or butter.
  3. Add the rutabagas and parsnips first. Cook for about 5–7 minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Next, add carrots and red onions. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add mushrooms, asparagus, and garlic. Sauté everything together for another 5–8 minutes until veggies are tender and golden.
  6. Stir in apricot jam and mix well to glaze the vegetables. Season with salt, pepper, and optional herbs.
  7. Remove from heat and serve warm as a side or main dish.

Tips

This dish pairs beautifully with rice, grilled chicken, or over quinoa. You can swap the apricot jam for peach or fig preserves for a different twist!
