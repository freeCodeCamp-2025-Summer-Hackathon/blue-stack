Pozole Verde

Description

This green pozole is a rich and savory Mexican soup made with tender chicken, hominy, and a bright green sauce of tomatillos, cilantro, and chiles. It’s topped with fresh cabbage, radishes, cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime, bold, fresh, and full of soul.

Ingredients

For the Soup Base

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breast
  • 1 (25 oz) can white hominy, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small white onion, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 cups water or chicken broth

For the Green Sauce

  • 8 tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 2 poblano peppers, seeded
  • 1 jalapeño or serrano pepper (optional, for spice)
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro (leaves and tender stems)
  • 1/2 cup chopped white onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds (pepitas), optional

Toppings

  • Shredded green cabbage
  • Thinly sliced radishes
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Crumbled cotija cheese or queso fresco
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Make the Soup Base

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken, onion, garlic, salt, and water or broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 25–30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  2. Remove chicken from the pot and shred it. Discard onion and garlic. Return shredded chicken to the pot along with the drained hominy.

2. Make the Green Sauce

  1. In a separate pot, boil tomatillos, poblanos, and jalapeño (if using) for 10 minutes until softened.
  2. Drain and transfer to a blender. Add cilantro, onion, garlic, and pumpkin seeds (if using). Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour the green sauce into the soup pot with the chicken and hominy. Simmer everything together for 15–20 minutes to blend the flavors. Add salt and pepper to taste.

3. Serve

  1. Ladle pozole into bowls.
  2. Top with shredded cabbage, radishes, fresh cilantro, cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
