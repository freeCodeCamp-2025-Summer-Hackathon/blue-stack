Candied Fire Bacon

Decritpion

Sweet, stikcy, and just spicy. This candied bacon is easy, irrestible treat. Perfect as a snack, brunch side, or even crumbled over deserts.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of bacon (any cut or thickness)
  • 3 Tablespoons hot honey
  • 3 Tablespoons brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil and arrange the bacon strips evenly
  3. Drizzle the hot honey over the bacon slices
  4. Sprinkle the brown sugar eavenly over each piece
  5. Bake for 2 minutes, or until bacon is carmelized and crisp
  6. Let cool slightly, the sugar will harden as it cools
  7. Serve and enjoy

Tips

For extra spicy, sprinkle crushed red pepper flakes before baking
