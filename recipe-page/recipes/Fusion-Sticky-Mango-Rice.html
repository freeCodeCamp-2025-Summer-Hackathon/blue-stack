Fusion Sticky Mango Rice

Description

A sweet, earthy, and lightly spiced dessert (or meal!) made with creamy coconut rice and fresh mango. Infused with cinnamon and coriander, this dish puts a unique spin on the classic Thai favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (sticky or jasmine recommended)
  • 1 fresh ripe mango, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 (13.5 oz) can coconut cream
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • Optional: extra cinnamon for topping

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and place it in a bowl with enough water to cover. Let it soak for about 2 hours to release starch and improve texture.
  2. Cook the rice using a rice cooker for best results, or follow the package instructions for stovetop preparation.
  3. Once the rice is fully cooked, stir in the coconut cream, sugar, ground cinnamon, and coriander while the rice is still warm. Mix until fully combined.
  4. Peel and slice the mango. Serve it alongside the sticky rice.
  5. Optional: sprinkle a little extra cinnamon on top before serving.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy this dish as a sweet dessert or turn it into a meal by pairing it with grilled or roasted chicken for a savory-sweet fusion twist.
